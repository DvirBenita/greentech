Discover Our Newest Module Partner

Greentech Renewables strives to be the most innovative solar distributor in North America by offering extensive on-site inventory, including a full selection of modules, inverters, racking, balance of systems, and storage equipment from the industry's leading manufacturers. In our continued efforts to support the unique needs of solar installers and developers, we seek opportunities to partner with trusted renewable energy leaders worldwide. 

Maxeon, one of our newest module partners, has a reputation for solar innovation, offering high-performance solar modules to countries across the globe. 

Solar panel efficiency is based on a few factors, including cell design, silicon type, and power output. The Maxeon 3 solar panel features a black frame and back sheet power output ranging from 405-420 watts and up to 22.2% efficiencies. The panel design was engineered to withstand all weather conditions with crack-resistant cells that are reinforced by solid connections protecting against corrosion and degradation. 

Design and efficiency greatly influence an installer’s choice when it comes to solar panels, but savings and warranty options can entice customers into choosing a specific product over competitors. Maxeon’s solar panels are backed by Maxeon’s industry-only 40-year warranty. This unique warranty is supported by extensive testing and field data from more than 33 million solar panels.

Warranty coverage includes: 

• Product: Product workmanship and materials for 40 years 
• Power: Highest warranted power output protection in the solar industry at 88.3% in year 40 
• Service: Repair, replacement, or refund for any defective panel for 40 years (removal, shipping, and installation included) 

One panel from each installation must be registered on the Maxeon website to access the complimentary extended warranty. If you’re interested in a comprehensive overview of selling tips, cost analysis, and product technology, view our recent introductory webinar. Maxeon 3 solar panels are now available exclusively through Greentech Renewables. Our locations are equipped with knowledgeable, experienced sales teams to support your quote requests, questions, and support needs.

Contact a location near you to place your Maxeon order today!
