Enphase MicroInverter 1.3kW on Flat Roof

This is a home installation on a carport in sunny Laguna Beach CA. It took only a day to install the solar PV system and it is generating roughly 80% of my monthly electrical load.

We've installed 6 Sharp ND224 solar panels with Enphase M190 microinverters and hooked it all up to an Enphase Envoy monitoring system.   The monitoring is amazing to watch as the system cranks out power.

I can monitor the power generation with Enphase Envoy even when I am away from the house.  Since it is a small beach house in Laguna I basically no longer have a monthly electric bill.
