Somersworth, NH, 7.14 kW Residential PV System

Installed in 2020 this project, located in Somersworth NH, features 21 solar panels. The solar panels are Panasonic VBHN340SA17 and they are paired with Enphase IQ7X microinverters. This 7.14 kW project was made possible thanks to the Greentech Renewables Auburn location and the hard work of NuWatt Energy LLC.

When talking with the homeowner they were extremely happy with the work of NuWatt Energy, "There are not a lot of words that can describe the entire process with NuWatt Energy. From the first call when I had a thousand questions to the quote phase and especially with the planning and installation, everything was flawless. I really can't find any negatives about the entire process.

We started the process in mid-September, so the chances to get the system installed and running by end of the year were quite slim due to the New England weather. However, they installed the panels and the entire system at the end of November and we started to produce energy by the end of December.‚Äù
