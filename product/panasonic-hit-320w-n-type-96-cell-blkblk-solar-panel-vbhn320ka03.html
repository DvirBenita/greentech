Description

Panasonic HIT 330W N-Type 96 Cell BLK/BLK Solar Panel, VBHN330KA03

The 96-cell high-efficiency HIT N330 solar panel provides a powerful combination of immediate energy savings, long-term performance, and sleek beauty. A remarkably low-temperature coefficient of -0.258%/°C helps generate greater electricity output even on the hottest days. Panasonic home solar panels are backed by an industry-leading parts and performance warranty that guarantees minimum power output of 90.76%after 25 years.

Features:
  • Enhanced New Frame Design (40mm)
  • Pyramid cell structure design for optimal sunlight capture
  • A low-temperature coefficient of -0.258%/°C produces higher power output at high temperatures
  • Heterojunction technology with ultra-thin amorphous silicon layers reduce electron loss
  • Water drainage frame design prevents rain accumulation, eliminates water stains from the panel surface
  • Complete 25-year warranty on linear power output, parts, and product workmanship
