Customer Portal

Located in El Paso, TX, Greentech Renewables El Paso is a trusted local resource for all of your solar equipment, product expertise, and sales support needs. From system design to commissioning, we’re here to help.  We are currently servicing El Paso and Southern New Mexico.  Take advantage of our local inventory, will call, and daily deliveries today!

 

Service Offerings

  • Finance - Project finance programs including Ensemble, Sunlight, Dividend, Concert, Direct Pay, Sungage, Sunnova, Goodleap. Learn more about our finance programs. 

  • Valet Service - Delivery of job material directly to the roof for efficient installation.

  • Design - Discounted design services for all solar projects. Learn more about our engineering services. 

  • Vendor Managed Inventory - Customized inventory management solution tailored to fit each customer's needs.

  • EV Chargers - Variety of level 2 chargers from Enel X Juice Box, Wallbox, Chargepoint, Loop, Emporia. Learn more about our electric vehicle charagers. 

  • Smart Home Kits – Easy install kit, Pro install kit.
