ProSolar

Professional Solar Products (ProSolar) has been a leader in the solar manufacturing industry for 28 years. It is the original US patent holder of the RoofTrac "Top-Down" solar mounting system and manufacturer of the industry leading GroundTrac ground mount system. 

ProSolar provides the best value to the solar industry in terms of quality, price, and service. All ProSolar products are Made in the USA, ensuring long-term product quality and safety. As an added assurance, all systems are fully engineered and tested. This has resulted in zero product failures, even through Florida hurricanes and major California earthquakes.
