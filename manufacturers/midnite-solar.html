MidNite Solar

MidNite Solar offers a wide range of grid tie and off grid solar PV equipments and solutions. Midnite Solar manufactures charge controllers, E-panels for inverters and charge controllers in addition to Midnite Solar AC, DC disconnects, DIN rail and panel mount breakers, string combiners, bus bars. Midnite Solar is among the most successful solar PV equipment suppliers of North America with a wide range of own-engineered balance of system products.
