SMA

SMA SOLAR TECHNOLOGY AG – ENERGY THAT CHANGES

As a leading global specialist for photovoltaic system technology, SMA is setting the standards today for the decentralized and renewable energy supply of tomorrow. More than 3,000 SMA employees in 20 countries have devoted themselves to this task. SMA's innovative solutions for all photovoltaic applications and SMA's unsurpassed service offer their customers worldwide greater independence in meeting their energy needs.

SERVICE SOLUTIONS TAILORED TO SUIT YOUR BUSINESS MODEL

We understand that not every plant owner and operator have the same service capabilities. SMA’s risk sharing scenarios for commercial plants are alternative options that allow plant owners and operators to choose what level of support they need, including self-performance if desired. This maximizes the unique business models of our customers. Each risk sharing scenario includes variances in:

  • Warranty length
  • Repair costs
  • Preventative maintenance
  • Plant-wide operations and maintenance

Residential & commercial system owners and installers benefit from a variety of unique SMA offerings, including SMA Smart Connected. This proactive solution speeds service for system owners while reducing truck rolls for installers by up to 50%.
