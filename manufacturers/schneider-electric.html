Schneider Electric

At Schneider Electric we are driving the digital transformation of modern homes with smart and sustainable energy solutions for ultimate comfort and energy efficiency. Our connected home ecosystem is a full suite of smart energy management. Solutions that connect you to your home's energy usage, from grid to plug.

  • Square D X Series Wiring Devices: This full line of switches, dimmers, sensors, and receptacles provide an easier way to remodel a whole home with fewer SKUs and more functionality. Available in traditional and connected devices that can be easily swapped for a new color or look, without rewiring, the Square D is setting the new standard in wiring devices. Now available and ready to ship in coinvent whole-home ready 30-packs!
  • Mobile apps: Our suite of mobile apps lets you quickly and easily monitor energy use, set automated lighting moods, and remotely control lights, outlets, and more.
  • Wiser Energy home power monitor: This connected energy monitoring system uses machine learning to understand how and when your home uses electricity, down to the device and appliance level, and presents the information via easy-to-understand visualizations in a mobile app so you can make wiser decisions about reducing your energy consumption. Installs in minutes into any panel with two available spaces.
  • Square D Energy Center: This smart electrical panel significantly simplifies adding solar, battery, generator, and EV charging to your home's electrical system and provides digital intelligence to help you improve electrical usage.
