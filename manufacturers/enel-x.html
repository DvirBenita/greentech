Enel X

Enel X is a global company with strong roots in the energy industry, combining the spirit of a startup with the infrastructure and expertise of a global innovator to provide smart, value-added services and solutions. Enel X e-Mobility is a market leader in designing and manufacturing smart electric vehicle (EV) charging stations. Its flagship product line expands upon the best-selling, JuiceBox smart charger, and provides installers cost-effective charging solutions for businesses to lower their upfront infrastructure investments and reduce their operating costs. Enel X operates over 130,000 electric vehicle smart charging ports in over 20 countries. In North America, Enel X serves around 4,500 business customers at more than 10,400 sites with dozens of utility smart charging programs expanding to serve EV drivers, businesses, and fleets.
