Roof Tech

Roof Tech Inc. is the first U.S. subsidiary of Yanegiken, renowned in Japan for more than four decades of innovative roofing technologies. We are the global pioneer in solar flexible flashing. We have been engineering solar mounting solutions since 1994 with flawless performance—over one million rooftop installations all over Japan and North America. Our waterproof unique PV mounting solutions include a 25-year warranty from the installation date that are IBC and IRC compliant for all asphalt, metal, EPDM, and TPO rooftops. Our national sales team provides superior service to installers and solar panel manufacturers throughout North America.
