SOLAR TOOLS USA

Solar Tools USA was born out of the frustrating lack of specialized tools for the solar industry and believes every trade should have access to the proper tools to be efficient and safe. You as a solar installer know there are many specific challenges found within a solar installation and we are on a mission to solve those problems for you! 

From residential to commercial, roof mount & ground mount solar projects, Solar Tools USA has a product designed specifically to assist you.

Faster. Safer. Easier is our mission.
