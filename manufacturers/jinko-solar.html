Jinko Solar

JinkoSolar (NYSE: JKS) is a leading PV module manufacturer and energy storage system integrator.  The company has deployed 100 GW in 160 countries globally, including more than 15 GW in the U.S.  EAGLE Modules come from facilities in Malaysia and Florida.  EAGLE Storage brings together the best energy storage technology for turnkey hardware and energy storage services.  Publicly vetted by trusted industry third party laboratories and top financial institutions, EAGLE is a trusted choice for U.S. solar and storage projects.  As a global leader with strong regional focus, JinkoSolar has more than 300 employees based in San Francisco, CA and Jacksonville, FL offices to provide timely, local service.
