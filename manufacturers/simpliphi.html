Simpliphi

Originally in the film and television industry, SimpliPhi was one LibertyPark Company and was involved in films and television series like Tron, Mad Men, Avatar, and Jarhead. Now, SimpliPhi focuses on energy storage and management solutions. Their batteries are lightweight, non-toxic, maintenance free, and provided seamless integration with renewable power sources in conjunction with or independent of the grid. SimpliPhi Batteries offer a longer life cycle, don’t build up excessive heat and don't risk dangerous thermal runaway. 

The potential global market for energy storage is vast.  And SimpliPhi Power is at the cutting edge - ready to provide breakthrough technology in multiple ways for businesses and consumers everywhere.  New game-changing products are coming.
