IronRidge

Founded in 1996, an IronRidge array is installed every 2.2 minutes. With nearly 500,000 installations across the country, IronRidge mounts have endured some of the most extreme weather events in history. Compatible with nearly every solar panel in the market, IronRidge manufactures and supports solutions for pitched roof, flat roof, and ground mount arrays for residential and commercial customers.
