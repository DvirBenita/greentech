Pegasus Solar

It started with a belief there was a better way to mount rooftop solar. Since our first sketches years ago, that passion has only grown. 

Today, Pegasus Solar is defined by continual improvement. We’re always looking for ways to advance mounting systems and the installation process. Reimagining. Strengthening. Simplifying. 

When a customer told us our products made for a “better day on the job,” a mantra was born. Because that’s what drives us to the shop in the morning: Finding ways to make every step of your experience better. From the warehouse to the rooftop. From the packaging and installation, through clean-up, inspection and a lifetime of great value.

It’s our mission to make rooftop solar easy.

We’re proud to be a growing American company committed to big ideas and customers like you.
