Yaskawa-Solectria Solar

Yaskawa - Solectria Solar is the leading U.S. commercial inverter supplier. Solectria’s products include 3.8 to 750 kilowatt (kW) inverters, string combiners and web-based monitoring for all sizes of solar photovoltaic (PV) systems. Yaskawa - Solectria Solar inverters have the highest quality, reliability and efficiency in the PV industry. 
 
Yaskawa – Solectria Solar’s PV inverters include 3.8-7.6 kW residential, single-phase, transformerless string inverters; 14-60 kW commercial, three-phase, transformerless string inverters; and 225kW-2 Megawatt (MW) commercial/utility-scale three-phase, central inverters for solar PV systems and grid-tied applications. Yaskawa -Solectria Solar was the first inverter manufacturer to introduce rapid shutdown solutions in compliance with NEC 2014. All of Solectria’s 3 phase central inverters are designed and manufactured in Lawrence, Massachusetts.
